About

  • Prototype made for the 3D Interaction and Virtual Reality course at UQAC
  • Explorium VR is an application for exploring environments such as the forest, the beach, etc. in Virtual Reality.
  • Date : from March to April 2023 (8 weeks)
  • Team composition : 3 developers
  • Keywords : Video Game, 3D, Virtual Reality, Exploration
  • Tools : Unity, C#, Oculus, Perforce, Rider

Abstract

ExploriumVR is a prototype VR exploration game for kids.
Players will be able to wander around the environment by picking up items in their inventory and completing missions.

My role

I participated in the development and programming of this game.
Voici les principales tâches dont je me suis occupée durant le projet :

Implementation of movements and interactions in VR

I implemented the movements and interactions in the environment using openXR. The prototype thus has movement by teleportation or continuous and as well as continuous or instantaneous rotation. The player can grab objects and interact with menus.

  • Teleportation

  • Interaction with objects

  • Interaction with menus

Inventory Implementation

I implemented the ability for the player to put items in an inventory and then pick them up.

  • Inventory menu

  • Inventory function

Implementation of Animals (AI)

I implemented for the first time an Artificial Intelligence. Indeed, an AI is used to control the movements of animals present in the environment.

  • Animal in the environment

  • Decision tree

  • Selecting a random move

Implementation of security instructions

Our application is intended for children, it was necessary to put in place safety instructions so that children do not reproduce the dangerous actions that they can do in the virtual environment in reality. For example: the child can touch and pick up different mushrooms, an indication therefore indicates that in reality they could be toxic and therefore be dangerous

  • Safety indication for mushrooms
