Forêt de Pan

Prototype created for the Serious Games course at UQAC.
Pan's Forest is a game designed to enhance auditory recognition of harmonic progressions.
Date: From March to April 2023 (8 weeks)
Team Composition: 5 developers and 1 music expert
Keywords: Serious game, 3D, Musical
My Role:

  • I was responsible for establishing the game's foundations by setting up the 3Cs (Camera, Character, Controller) and managing input using Unity's 'New Inputs Manager.'
  • I also implemented a core mechanic that allows players to calm animals using a combo, along with the implementation of audio and visual feedback.
  • Furthermore, I implemented Artificial Intelligence (AI) to control animal movements within the virtual environment.
  • Additionally, I explored the Mixamo tool to integrate various character animations, enhancing player immersion and interaction in the game.

AZDH

Prototype created for the Network Programming in Game Development course at UQAC.
AZDH is a multiplayer PVE TPS (Third-Person Shooter) where players must survive waves of enemies.
Date: From March to April 2023 (8 weeks)
Team Composition: 3 developers
Keywords: Video game, 3D, Multiplayer, PVE, TPS
My Role:

  • I created the wave system and drone spawning, where a new wave starts every 60 seconds, leading to the appearance of enemy drones. If a wave is defeated in less than 60 seconds, the excess time is carried over to the next wave, and this data is synchronized among players.
  • I also implemented statistics (Stats) and achievements (Achievements) using the Epic Online Services (EOS), providing objectives and rewards to players.
