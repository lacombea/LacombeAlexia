About

  • Project realized for the OOP course and the "Honest Engineer" project in 3rd year of ENIB
  • The aim of this game is to demystify the bad image of sharks created by the cinema and other media through a puzzles game.
  • Date : from April to May 2021 (8 weeks)
  • Team composition : 2 developers
  • Keywords : Video Game, Educational Game, Sharks
  • Tools : Python, Tkinter, GitHub

Introduction and first 2 puzzles

Gameplay

The player will have to solve different puzzles about sharks with the help of the available clues and directions.

After solving a puzzle, a screen with additional information about the solution will appear.
