Abstract

AZDH is a prototype network shooter in which players must survive waves of enemy drones. Drones can spawn ammo which will be useful to players.
This prototype uses Epic Online Services (EOS) to manage lobbies and online achievements.

My role

I participated in the development and programming of this game. Here are the main tasks I took care of during the project:

Creation of the wave system and spawning of drones

I implemented the wave system: after 60 seconds a new wave starts and enemy drones spawn. If a wave is defeated in less than 60 seconds: the extra time is kept and the next wave starts.
The time as well as the number of drones are of course replicated between the players.

Implementation of Stats and Achievements

I implemented Stats and Achievements using Epic Online Services (EOS).
