Abstract

Flickering Shadows is a top-down fixed camera 3D puzzle game.
Play as a young girl from the school's Occult Science Club who seeks to complete the ritual to unleash a powerful demo. To achieve this, you will have to cross a gloomy dungeon plunged into darkness while carrying your candle to the place of the ritual. However, beware of shadow creatures who will seek to extinguish your flame as soon as your back is turned!
