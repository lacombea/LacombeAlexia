Abstract

Cogni-Actif is a game aimed at allowing primary school students to perform daily physical activity while solving math or French exercises for example.
To do this, the students follow the movements made by a robot on the screen and must do enough physical activity to fill up its battery.

My role

I participated in the development and programming of this game. Here are the main tasks I took care of during the project:

Implementation of statistics and the bank

I implemented everything related to player statistics and in-game currency. The prototype thus has displays of various statistics on physical activity. By practicing the activity, the players earn DBs (game currencies), at the end of the mini-game or thanks to a wheel of luck if the teacher considers that they have answered the questions well enough and carried out the movements.
